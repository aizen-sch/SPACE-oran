Types of Galaxies:

1. Types of Galaxies: - Spiral Galaxies: Have spiral arms extending from their centers, like the Milky Way. - Elliptical Galaxies: Have an oval or spherical shape and contain old stars. - Irregular Galaxies:Lack a defined shape and often result from collisions or interactions between galaxies. 2. Our Galaxy: The Milky Way: - A spiral galaxy containing hundreds of billions of stars. - Our solar system is located in one of its arms, known as the "Orion Arm." - It contains a supermassive black hole at its center called "Sagittarius A." 3.Other Galaxies: -Andromeda: The closest spiral galaxy to the Milky Way, located about 2.5 million light-years away. -Triangulum Galaxy
