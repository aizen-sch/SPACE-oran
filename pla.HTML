SUN

Of course! Here's a brief summary of the most important facts about the Sun: 1. Basic Characteristics: - A yellow dwarf star, about (4.6 billion years old) - Its mass is 333,000 times that of Earth, and its diameter is 109 times that of Earth. - Surface temperature: 5,500°C, core temperature: (15 million°C) 2.Composition: - 74% hydrogen, (24% helium), (2% other elements) 3. Energy: - Produces energy through nuclear fusion (converting hydrogen into helium). - Releases energy as light and heat that supports life on Earth. 4. Atmosphere: - Consists of the photosphere (the visible layer), the chromosphere, and the solar corona. 5. Solar Activity: - Includes sunspots, solar flares, and solar wind. 6. Role in the Solar System: - It keeps the planets in their orbits and provides light and heat. 7. Future: - After (5 billion years), it will expand into a red giant and then turn into a white dwarf.
