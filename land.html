Planet Earth

1. Physical Properties: - Shape:Oblate spheroid (bulged at the equator and flattened at the poles). - Diameter: Approximately 12,742 km. - Area: 510 million km². - Volume:Approximately 1 trillion km³. - Mass: Approximately 5.97 × 10^24 kg. 2. Internal Structure: - Crust: The solid outer layer, composed of solid rock. - Mantle: A thick layer of partially molten rock. - Core: Consists of two parts, the outer core (liquid) and the inner core (solid), composed mainly of iron and nickel.
