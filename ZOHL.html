Saturn

1. Location and Size: - Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun. - It is considered the second largest planet in the solar system after Jupiter. - Its diameter is approximately 116,460 km, which is very large compared to Earth. 2. Famous Rings: - Saturn is famous for its spectacular rings, which are composed of ice, rock, and dust particles. - These rings are visible from Earth even through a small telescope. - The rings are divided into several groups, the most famous of which are the A, B, and C rings. 3. Composition and Atmosphere: - Saturn is a gas giant planet, composed mostly of hydrogen and helium. - Its atmosphere contains distinctive cloud bands, but they are less pronounced than those on Jupiter. 4.
